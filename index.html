About

BEEODA is a suit of open-source software and educational materials for processing and analyzing earth observation data. It contains a range of open-source image processing tools such as QGIS, Orfeo, GRASS, R, etc. It is developed and maintained by Dr. Pontus Olofsson, Chris Holden and Eric Bullock [hyperlink CVs?], who are all affiliated with the Department of Earth & Environment at Boston University. To explore BEEODA, please download it from our GitHub repository.

Services

We offer to teach workshops using the BEEODA software and material for analyzing earth observation data. Workshop topics may include satellite image classification of land cover, object-based classification of high-resolution imagery, change detection, accuracy and area estimation.
